(myDefinitionofPreOrder)=
**A way to think about a preorder:** A preorder can be thought of as a hierarchy that determines between what states an allowed operation exists, and hence what states are more resourceful then others.

(static_resource_theory)=
**Static Resource Theory:** A resource theory where the underlying objects are quantum states.

(dynamical_resource_theory)=
**Dynamic Resource Theory:** A resource theory where the underlying objects are quantum channels.
